The California Hamster Association
We are a non-profit hamster club in Southern California dedicated to educating Syrian and Dwarf hamster owners and to raising the quality of hamsters in California and North America. Our shows and this web site are integral parts of this mission.  We had our first show on May 13, 2000 and have about six shows a year.  Everyone is welcome to attend a show.

You may reach us at our club e-mail address:  calhamassoc@hotmail.com
or we welcome you at any of our shows.
