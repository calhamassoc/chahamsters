CHA Logo Contest
We complete our logo contest and decided to use two logos.  We are using logo #5 for our literature and using the wheel logos for t-shirts and other items.
This idea does not yet have a graphic.  If you design the graphics for this logo and this logo is chosen, both the person with the idea and the person who designed the graphics will receive a free T-shirt.

Idea
Circle that has obviously been chewed out by a hamster, all raggedy.  Inside the circle is a simple line drawing of a hamster sitting on its chubby little bottom like a shmoo (if you are old enough to remember Al Capp).  Hamster is wearing a tiny tee shirt with the letters CHA on the front of it.
